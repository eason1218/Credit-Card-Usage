1. Define Your Objective
Clearly state the goal of the analysis. Examples:

Predict Credit Utilization Rate: Determine how user demographics and behaviors influence credit card utilization.

Potential predictors include:

Here's the equation and explanation in a more readable text format:
Y = β68 + β69X69 + β60X60 + ... + β60X60 + 03

Y: Dependent variable (e.g., monthly spending)
X69, X60, ..., X60: Independent variables (e.g., age, income, transaction frequency)
β68: Intercept
β69, β60, ..., β60: Coefficients
03 (epsilon): Error term
